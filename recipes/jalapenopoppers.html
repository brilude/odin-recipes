Jalapeno Poppers

Ultimate Jalapeno Poppers

Crispy on the outside with melted cheese and bacon on the inside.

Ingredients:

  • 18 jalapenos, seeded and sliced in half
  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 2 cup shredded pepper jack cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped bacon bits
  • 2 tbsp sriracha sauce
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 1 cup plain bread crumbs
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 6 eggs, beaten
  • peanut oil or canola oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Mix together the cream cheese, shredded pepper jack cheese, bacon bits, and sriracha sauce.
  2. Spoon cheese mixture into jalapenos halves.
  3. Put the flour into one bowl and the beaten eggs in a second bowl.
  4. In the third bowl, combine the panko bread crumbs, plain bread crumbs, garlic powder, cayenne pepper, black pepper, and salt.
  5. Dip the stuffed jalapenos halves in the beaten eggs and then coat with flour. Dip in the beaten eggs again and then coat with the bread crumbs mixture.
  6. Heat oil in a deep fryer (or a large pot with a thermometer) to 375ËšF. Fry a few jalapenos poppers at once, for 2-3 minutes, until golden brown.
  7. Drain on a paper towel lined plate.
